### Bulk Remote Activation

1. Select PCs from list (or "Select All Expiring")
2. Click **"Bulk Activate"**
3. Choose activation method (Smart recommended)
4. Click **"Start"**
5. Progress shows in real-time

### Compliance Reports

1. Click **"Reports"**
2. Select report type:
   - Full License Inventory
   - Expiring Licenses (<30 days)
   - Expired Licenses
   - Compliance Summary
3. Choose format: PDF, CSV, Excel
4. Click **"Generate"**
5. Export for auditors or management

### Email Alerts

1. Click **"Alerts"** → **"Configure"**
2. Enter SMTP settings
3. Set thresholds (30, 15, 7, 1 days)
4. Add recipient emails
5. Click **"Save"**
6. Automatic notifications on expiration

### Automated Remediation

Enable auto-remediation to fix issues automatically:

- Renew expiring licenses (<30 days)
- Retry failed activations (3 attempts)
- Restart stalled licensing services
- Replace invalid product keys
